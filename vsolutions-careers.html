— Common Questions

Hiring FAQ.

Are roles fully remote?
Most roles offer fully remote options with flexible hours. Hybrid is available for Macomb, MI candidates. We hire international talent — strong English proficiency required for client-facing roles.
Do you offer freelance-to-full-time conversions?
Yes. Several roles (Content Writer, Graphic Designer, Video Editor, Digital Marketing Specialist, SEO) start as freelance and convert to full-time based on fit and performance — typically within 60-90 days.
What's the interview process?
1) Apply via email or form → 2) Initial 15-min screen call → 3) Technical/portfolio review → 4) Team interview → 5) Paid trial project (1-3 days) → 6) Offer. Typically 1-2 weeks end-to-end.
What does compensation look like?
Base salary at market rates plus performance bonuses tied to real outcomes — campaign ROI for marketing roles, ranking/traffic improvements for SEO, content impact for writers, uncapped commissions for sales. Compensation is discussed openly during the process.
I don't see a role that fits — can I still apply?
Absolutely. Email careers@vsolutionsinc.com with your resume and a short note explaining your skills. We're always happy to hear from talented people, even if there's no current opening for your specialty.
